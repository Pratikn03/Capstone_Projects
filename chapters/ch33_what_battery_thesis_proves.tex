\chapter{What the Monograph Proves and What It Does Not}
\label{ch:what-battery-thesis-proves}

The monograph now treats battery as the deepest witness row inside a
universal-first book, not as the conceptual center of the project. This
chapter states the resulting proof boundary precisely.

\section{Central Theorem Contribution}

The central theorem contribution is a domain-agnostic degraded-observation
safety kernel:

\begin{enumerate}
  \item the observation--action gap is the right control object;
  \item reliability-aware uncertainty inflation and safe-action repair are not
    cosmetic additions but structural requirements under degraded telemetry;
  \item the battery row supplies the deepest complete theorem-to-code-to-artifact
    witness for that kernel;
  \item the universality extension is formal only where the current transfer
    obligations and replay surfaces are actually discharged.
\end{enumerate}

\section{Empirical Meaning}

The empirical package supports the theorem layer in a stratified way:

\begin{enumerate}
  \item battery is the deepest witness row;
  \item industrial, healthcare, and the bounded AV contract defend the same
    runtime grammar under their current parity gates;
  \item navigation and aerospace mark the current frontier rather than
    completed equal-peer closure.
\end{enumerate}

\section{What the Result Does Not Claim}

The monograph does \emph{not} claim:

\begin{enumerate}
  \item equal-domain proof closure across all six rows,
  \item universal deployment readiness,
  \item adversarial completeness outside the stated degradation model,
  \item or a replacement for native barrier, MPC, or viability methods in
    their strongest domain-specific settings.
\end{enumerate}

\section{Scope of the Final Claim}

The final claim is therefore:

\begin{quote}
Under degraded telemetry, observed safety can diverge from true physical
safety. ORIUS addresses this gap with a domain-agnostic repair-and-certify
kernel built on reliability-aware uncertainty inflation, safe-action repair,
fallback semantics, and causal runtime certificates. The book is
universal-first in structure, but evidence promotion remains governed by one
explicit parity matrix.
\end{quote}

\section{Summary}

The monograph is broad because it organizes one universal safety layer across
multiple domains. It is defensible because it keeps equal-domain closure tied
to explicit evidence gates rather than treating every row as already equivalent
to the witness surface.
