
\chapter{ORIUS Program Architecture and Remaining Frontier}
\label{ch:roadmap}

This chapter no longer serves as a speculative future roadmap.  The canonical
thesis has already consolidated ORIUS as a program-first dissertation, so the
purpose here is different: to state how Papers~1--6 fit together as one
research program, to show which parts of that program are reference versus
bounded layers, and to isolate the open frontier that remains after the
current package.

\section{Paper~1 as the Reference Scientific Witness}

Paper~1 remains the scientific anchor of the dissertation.  It establishes the
battery-domain OASG witness, the T1--T8 theorem ladder, the locked
theorem-to-code-to-artifact lineage, and the strongest causal statement of the
work: under degraded telemetry, a controller can be observationally safe while
physically unsafe, and the DC\textsuperscript{3}S repair-and-certify kernel
can remove that hidden violation surface on the battery reference row.

\section{Papers~2--6 as the Explicit Second Thesis Spine}

The dissertation is therefore not battery-only.  It has a second explicit
program spine:

\begin{enumerate}
  \item \textbf{Paper~2: temporal validity.}  This layer contributes
    certificate horizon, expiration, and blackout safe-hold semantics.  Its
    status is a bounded temporal theorem extension over the battery ladder.
  \item \textbf{Paper~3: graceful fallback.}  This layer contributes the
    canonical four-policy degradation benchmark under prolonged blindness.
    Its status is a bounded empirical benchmark surface rather than a general
    fallback-dominance theorem.
  \item \textbf{Paper~4: ORIUS-Bench.}  This layer contributes replayability,
    schema discipline, metric discipline, and multi-domain benchmark
    reproducibility.  Its status is a benchmark-contract contribution.
  \item \textbf{Paper~5: bounded composition.}  This layer contributes the
    shared-feeder non-composition counterexample and coordinated repair
    protocols.  Its status is a bounded composition extension, not a full
    composition theorem.
  \item \textbf{Paper~6: runtime governance.}  This layer contributes
    certificate lifecycle, fallback, audit continuity, and recovery as
    explicit runtime semantics.  Its status is a bounded
    runtime-governance extension.
\end{enumerate}

\section{Layer Dependency Order}

These layers do not carry the same proof burden, but they do depend on one
another in a strict order:

\begin{enumerate}
  \item Paper~1 provides the battery witness and the theorem-facing kernel.
  \item Papers~2 and~3 extend that same battery-centered kernel temporally and
    operationally.
  \item Paper~4 turns the evaluation surface into a reusable benchmark
    contract.
  \item Paper~5 adds a bounded multi-agent coordination layer on top of local
    certificate semantics.
  \item Paper~6 adds bounded runtime governance around issuance, validation,
    expiry, fallback, audit, and recovery.
\end{enumerate}

Read in that order, the ORIUS program is already a coherent dissertation
architecture rather than a queue of future work items.

\section{What Remains Open After the Current Program}

The remaining gaps are now frontier gaps, not missing Papers~2--6 layers:

\begin{enumerate}
  \item \textbf{Autonomous vehicles.}  AV remains proof-candidate because the
    current repair surface does not yet close the constraint-class mismatch.
  \item \textbf{Navigation.}  Navigation remains shadow-synthetic until its
    real-data trainable row and defended telemetry path are complete.
  \item \textbf{Aerospace.}  Aerospace remains experimental until the replay
    surface and promotion contract are strengthened beyond the current
    experimental row.
  \item \textbf{Deployment beyond bounded runtime governance.}  CertOS
    currently closes bounded lifecycle, audit, fallback, and recovery
    semantics.  Full field deployment remains outside the present claim
    boundary.
\end{enumerate}

\section{What This Chapter Contributes}

The role of this chapter is therefore architectural rather than speculative.
It makes the current thesis easier to defend by separating three things that
older drafts blurred together:

\begin{enumerate}
  \item the \textbf{reference proof witness} (Paper~1),
  \item the \textbf{bounded defended program layers} (Papers~2--6),
  \item and the \textbf{open frontier rows} (AV, navigation, aerospace, and
    field deployment).
\end{enumerate}

That separation is the honest program-first stance of the final dissertation.
