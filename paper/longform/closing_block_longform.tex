\section{Closing ORIUS Monograph Block}\label{sec:closing_longform}

The closing block below brings the full monograph claim boundary directly into
the canonical paper build. This keeps \texttt{paper.pdf} honest about what the
battery witness establishes, what the defended bounded rows establish across
other domains, and what still remains outside the defended evidence surface.

\paragraph{Evidence hierarchy and universality boundary.}
ORIUS is already universal at the architecture layer, but equal-domain parity
remains a governed target rather than a present-tense fact.  The evidence
hierarchy in this monograph is:
\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=*,topsep=2pt,itemsep=1pt]
  \item \textbf{Battery} remains the witness row with the deepest theorem-to-
        artifact closure and the strongest replay plus economics linkage.
  \item \textbf{AV, industrial, and healthcare} are defended bounded rows:
        they clear the shared runtime and benchmark contract, but they do not
        inherit the full witness-level proof closure of battery.
  \item \textbf{Navigation} remains a shadow-synthetic row.  It demonstrates
        adapter and protocol portability, but not defended real-data parity.
  \item \textbf{Aerospace} remains experimental.  The adapter surface exists,
        but the promotion contract still requires a stronger real-task closure.
\end{enumerate}
Temporal validity, graceful fallback, benchmark discipline, bounded
composition, and runtime governance are all part of the defended ORIUS
surface, but none of them upgrades bounded evidence into equal-domain theorem
closure.  Battery remains the only full witness row in the current release.
