\section{Editorial Ledgers}
\label{sec:ieee-app-ledgers}

The flagship IEEE draft is accompanied by a private claim-delta ledger and a revision
ledger. They are not part of the scientific evidence surface, but they are useful for
editing discipline because they distinguish between claims already supported by tracked
repo artifacts, claims supported primarily by literature, and claims that still require
closure work before an artifact-strict submission.
