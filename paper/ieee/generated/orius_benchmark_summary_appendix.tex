\paragraph{Writing-gap synthesis.}
The benchmark corpus pushes the ORIUS flagship draft toward five consistent writing upgrades: lead with the field-level problem instead of artifact logistics; state one dominant technical move early; compress evidence to a few decisive surfaces; keep limitations adjacent to the main claim; and make the conclusion argue for a field object rather than merely restating chapter coverage.
