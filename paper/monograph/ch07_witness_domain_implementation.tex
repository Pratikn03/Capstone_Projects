\section{Why battery is the witness domain}
Battery remains the deepest witness row because it carries the strongest
theorem-to-code-to-artifact lineage in the repository. That makes it the right place to
show the full ORIUS stack end to end without pretending that the dissertation is only
about energy systems. The witness-domain role is therefore methodological: battery is the
row where the runtime contract, theorem ladder, replay protocol, and claim-lock pipeline
are all simultaneously visible.

\section{Data and dataset cards}
The battery surface is built from tracked regional energy and telemetry artifacts rather
than from a single benchmark file committed to git. The dissertation keeps the data story
artifact-first and manifest-driven so that large external datasets can still participate in
a reproducible thesis pipeline.

\section{Forecasting, calibration, and reliability surfaces}
The witness row deliberately separates the engineered forecasting track from the
degraded-observation novelty track. The current engineered track remains a strong systems
baseline, while the learned-reliability and raw-sequence surfaces are framed as bounded
deep-learning novelty inside ORIUS rather than as a replacement thesis identity.

\section{Control baselines and adapter implementation}
Battery is also the cleanest row for showing how the adapter binds state semantics,
uncertainty construction, action repair, fallback, and certificate emission to a real
plant envelope. The dissertation uses that row to explain the implementation discipline:
nominal forecasting and control are replaceable, but the ORIUS adapter boundary and replay
contract remain fixed.

\section{Locked artifact protocol}
The witness row is defended through locked artifacts rather than through narrative memory.
Training surfaces, replay outputs, theorem traces, calibration diagnostics, and runtime
tables all point back to generated publication assets or manifests. This is the point at
which the chapter-first dissertation model and the repository's claim-lock machinery meet:
the prose is curated, but the evidence remains machine-governed.
