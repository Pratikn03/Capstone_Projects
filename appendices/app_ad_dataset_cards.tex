\chapter{Dataset Cards}
\label{app:dataset-cards}

This appendix indexes the source dataset cards that govern the active ORIUS
data surfaces.  The canonical human-readable cards live under
\path{docs/data_cards/}; the purpose of this appendix is to bind those cards
into the thesis control path without duplicating the full markdown bodies in
multiple places.

\noindent Each card records source provenance, split policy, preprocessing,
known issues, and the exact role the dataset plays in the defended ORIUS
claim surface.  The cards are intended to be updated alongside the raw-data
manifests and release-manifest refresh pipeline.
