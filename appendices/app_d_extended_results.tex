\section{Metric Definitions}

For completeness, the extended metrics reported above are:
\begin{description}[style=nextline]
  \item[TSVR] True-SOC Violation Rate (Appendix~\ref{app:notation}).
  \item[IR] Intervention Rate: fraction of steps where the shield
    modifies the candidate action.
  \item[PICP] Prediction-Interval Coverage Probability of the conformal
    interval on held-out data.
  \item[Sev.\,$p_{95}$] 95th percentile of the violation severity
    distribution; zero when TSVR\,$=$\,0.
  \item[Revenue] Normalised arbitrage revenue over the evaluation
    horizon.
  \item[Latency] Median per-step wall-clock time including OQE,
    uncertainty inflation, shield, and certificate emission.
\end{description}
