\section{Paper Section Reference}

The conference paper (IEEE two-column format) sections map roughly as follows: Introduction (ch01--ch02), Problem Formulation (ch03--ch04), Methods (ch05--ch10), Results (ch11--ch14). Theorems T1--T4 appear in the paper's theoretical section and map to ch16--ch19. Extension results (ch21--ch32) appear in the paper's evaluation and discussion sections where space permits.
