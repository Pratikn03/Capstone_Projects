\chapter{Integrated Theorem Surface Register}
\label{app:integrated-theorem-surface-register}

This appendix audits the full integrated theorem-like surface of the thesis
rather than only the flagship battery ladder.  The T1--T8 ladder in
Chapters~\ref{ch:theorem-oasg}--\ref{ch:temporal-extensions} remains the
canonical proof spine for the battery safety argument, and
Chapter~\ref{ch:universality-completeness} adds the later T9--T11 extension.
Together they sit inside a broader integrated surface that also includes the
precursor results in Part~I, supporting lemmas, propositions, corollaries,
definitions, and the standing assumption register.

The register below is generated from the YAML-canonical theorem registry by
\path{scripts/build_theorem_surface_register.py}.  It is intended to answer a
simple governance question without ambiguity: which theorem-like statements are
present in the active thesis, where do they live, and which ones belong to the
defended unique theorem program as opposed to the supporting integrated
surface.  The older hard-gated 18-row release status is reported in
Appendix~\ref{app:verified-theorems-gap-audit}; this appendix records the full
inventory that the release gate audits.

The surface counts are summarised in Table~\ref{tab:integrated-theorem-surface-summary}
(Chapter~\ref{ch:assumptions}).
